How Campus SkillSwap Works

Simple steps to start your learning journey

1

Register & Verify

Sign up with your university email and verify your student status. Get 2 free Time Credits to start!

2

List Your Skills

Add skills you can teach - from academics to arts, coding to cooking. Set your availability and preferences.

3

Browse & Request

Explore the marketplace, find skills you want to learn, and request sessions with other students.

4

Learn & Earn

Attend sessions, teach others, and watch your Time Credits grow. Build your reputation through reviews.
