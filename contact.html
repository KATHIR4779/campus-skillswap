Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Campus SkillSwap

How do I verify my university email?

After registering with your university email, you'll receive a verification link. Click the link to confirm your student status. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do Time Credits work?

Time Credits are our currency system. You earn 1 credit for every hour you teach, and spend credits to learn from others. New users get 2 free credits to start. Credits are automatically transferred after session completion.

What if I need to cancel a session?

You can cancel sessions up to 2 hours before the scheduled time without penalty. Cancellations within 2 hours may result in credit deduction. Contact support for emergency cancellations.

How are meeting locations determined?

We provide a list of safe, public locations on campus like the library, student common areas, and department lobbies. Both parties must agree on the location before the session.

Can I teach multiple skills?

Absolutely! You can list as many skills as you're comfortable teaching. Each skill can have different pricing and availability. This helps you reach more students and earn more credits.

What if I have a bad experience with a session?

We take user safety and satisfaction seriously. You can report issues through the platform, leave honest reviews, and contact support. We investigate all reports and take appropriate action.
