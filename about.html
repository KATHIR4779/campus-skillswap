About Campus SkillSwap

Empowering students to learn and teach through peer-to-peer skill exchange

Our Mission

Campus SkillSwap is a revolutionary platform designed exclusively for college students in Coimbatore. We believe that every student has valuable skills to share and the right to learn without financial barriers.

Our mission is to create a symbiotic ecosystem where time becomes currency, knowledge flows freely, and students from different departments, years, and backgrounds come together to learn and grow.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accessibility

We believe education should be accessible to all students, regardless of their financial situation. Time Credits ensure everyone can participate.

Community

We foster a strong sense of community where students support each other's growth and build lasting connections across campus.

Trust & Safety

University email verification and a robust rating system ensure a safe, trustworthy environment for all interactions.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to provide the best learning experience, from AI-powered matching to seamless session coordination.

Local Focus

We celebrate and preserve local culture by promoting traditional skills like Carnatic music, Tamil language, and Kongu cuisine.

Excellence

We maintain high standards through our rating system and community guidelines, ensuring quality learning experiences.

How Campus SkillSwap Works

A simple, effective system for skill exchange

1

Register & Verify

Sign up with your university email address. We verify your student status and give you 2 free Time Credits to get started.

  • University email verification
  • Profile creation with major and year
  • 2 free Time Credits
2

List Your Skills

Add skills you can teach to other students. Set your availability, pricing in Time Credits, and describe what you'll cover.

  • Add multiple skills
  • Set availability and pricing
  • Write detailed descriptions
3

Browse & Request

Explore the marketplace to find skills you want to learn. Filter by category, search by keyword, and request sessions.

  • Advanced search and filtering
  • View teacher profiles and ratings
  • Request sessions with preferred times
4

Learn & Earn

Attend sessions, teach others, and watch your Time Credits grow. Leave reviews to help build the community.

  • Safe meeting locations on campus
  • Mandatory reviews and ratings
  • Time Credits automatically transferred
